Fact-Check Guide

The SIFT Method

A structured four-step model for checking digital claims before you trust or share them.

S

Stop

Pause before reacting. Ask whether you recognize the source, author, and context.

I

Investigate

Look beyond the screenshot or post. Search for the publisher, date, and original statement.

F

Find

Search for reporting from credible outlets, experts, or official institutions.

T

Trace

Follow quotes, images, and claims back to their earliest source to catch context loss.
