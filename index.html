Method

How Arixion verifies

Arixion AI combines layered analysis with the SIFT approach to help you verify before you share.

1

Stop

Pause before sharing. Ask whether the claim is designed to trigger a strong, fast reaction.

2

Investigate

Look at the source. Does it have clear ownership, editorial standards, and a track record?

3

Find

Search for other coverage. Can you find multiple credible outlets confirming the same core facts?

4

Trace

Go back to the original context. Has the quote, image, or statistic been cropped or reframed?
